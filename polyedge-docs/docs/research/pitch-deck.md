## Three Safety Mechanisms

### Bounded Autonomy
Hard deterministic gates — risk profiles that cannot be bypassed, promotion pipeline with statistical gates, $10/day LLM budget cap, experiment isolation.

### StrategyGenome
Chromosomal strategy representation with five chromosomes (Perception, Cognition, Execution, Risk, Meta). Genetic operators evolvestrategies based on composite fitness.

### Dual-Debate Validation
Bull vs Bear agents argue before a Judge. Confidence threshold gates capital deployment. Every verdict recorded in TradeAttempt ledger.
